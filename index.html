Our Mission/Vision

Mission

We are committed in providing our customers and partners with the best quality agricultural products, farm inputs and services in the most cost competitive way possible. We shall prudently exercise responsibility in caring for the welfare of our stakeholders supported by a culture of innovation, collaboration and excellence in our organization. We shall endeavor to significantly contribute in uplifting the economic and social condition of the people in the towns, cities and provinces where our businesses are situated.

Vision

M3C shall be the dominant distributor of multi-brand animal products such as feeds, vaccines, medicines and supplements in Leyte within the next 5 years. We shall established a complete farm-to-table business operation in hog farming by 2029. We shall pursue potentials in crop farming and develop an integrated farming project by 2025.

About Us

M3C General Foods, Inc. started as a small business in 2019, M3C became a distributor of Feedpro natural hog feed products to areas in Eastern Leyte. In 2020, the company expanded its portfolio by adding Pro Natural Broiler Feeds and Rabbit Feeds to its roster of products. By becoming a distributor of Sunjin Philippines, we further added Sunjin hog, layering and game fowl feeds to our products. In the same year, M3C widened its operations by serving and adding value to farmers and feed stations in Tacloban. With a bias for growth, M3C is now also a distributor of Pet One pet food products and SANTEH Aqua Feeds. At the same time, M3C has expanded its service and distribution to Eastern Samar as well.
